Sreelekha Jayaram

An accomplished and passionate artist from Kerala, Sreelekha Jayaram has carved a distinguished path in the world of visual arts through dedication, innovation, and exceptional creativity. With expertise spanning multiple mediums and a remarkable collection of accolades, she continues to inspire and captivate through her work.

Specialized in: Acrylic Painting • Stencil Art • Pencil Sketching • Contemporary Art Forms

From childhood onwards, Sreelekha has been a consistent achiever, collecting multiple awards and recognitions from schools, universities, and prestigious art competitions across Kerala. Her work embodies a unique blend of traditional artistry and modern creativity.
